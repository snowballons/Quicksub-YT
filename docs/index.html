How to Use

1

Add Channels

Upload a .csv/.txt file, paste comma-separated channel URLs, or enter a webpage URL to scan for links.

2

Start Processing

Click "Start Processing". The extension will open each channel page one by one.

3

Subscribe Manually

You'll have a 10-second window on each channel page to click the "Subscribe" button yourself.

4

Cooldown & Reset

Respect session limits. After processing a batch or using your session allowance, cooldowns apply. Inactivity resets your session!

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how YT Channel Importer ("the Extension"), developed by snowballons ("we," "us," or "our"), handles your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The core functionality of the YT Channel Importer extension is designed to operate locally on your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following personal information through the extension itself:

  • Your YouTube browsing history or watch history.
  • Your YouTube account credentials.
  • The content of the files (CSV, TXT) you upload, beyond local processing within your browser session.
  • The specific URLs of webpages you scan, beyond local processing within your browser session to extract links.
  • The list of YouTube channels you process or subscribe to.

2. Information Processed Locally

The following data is processed and stored locally on your device using browser storage (`chrome.storage.local`) for the Extension's functionality:

  • Usage State: This includes `linksUsedInSession`, `lastActivityTimestamp`, and `hardCooldownUntil` to manage the free tier usage limits and session resets.

This locally stored data is not transmitted to us or any third parties by the Extension.

3. Information Collected During Purchase (via Third-Party Processor)

If you choose to purchase an "Advanced Version" license key, the payment process is handled by our third-party payment processor (Paddle). The information you provide during checkout (such as your name, email address, and payment details) is collected and processed by the payment processor according to their own privacy policy. We may receive transactional information from the payment processor (e.g., purchase confirmation, customer email for key delivery) to fulfill your order and provide support.

4. Webpage Scanning Feature (`<all_urls>` permission)

The "Scan Webpage for Channels" feature requires the `<all_urls>` host permission. This permission is used solely to allow the Extension to fetch the HTML content of the webpage URL you explicitly provide in the Extension's popup. The content is fetched and processed locally within your browser (using an offscreen document) to identify potential YouTube channel links. The content of the webpage or the URL itself is not stored by us or transmitted to our servers by the Extension.
